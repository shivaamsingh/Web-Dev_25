Block Elements (Most Commonly Used First)

<div>: A generic container for flow content. 
<p>: Represents a paragraph of text.
<h1> to <h6>: Headings, with <h1> being the highest level and <h6> the lowest.
<ul>: An unordered list
<ol>: An ordered list.
<li>: A list item, used within <ul> or <ol>.
<form>: A section containing from controls.
<table>: A table, used to display tabular data.
<section>: A stand-alone section of a document.
